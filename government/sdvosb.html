Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business.

ColeHart Enterprises is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB). This designation enables federal agencies to fulfill set-aside contracting requirements while partnering with a firm that brings real operational and technical expertise.

The Designation

What SDVOSB means for your program.

SDVOSB set-asides provide agencies with a straightforward path to meeting small business contracting goals without sacrificing capability.

ColeHart combines the set-aside eligibility agencies need with the infrastructure, technology, and program management expertise their missions demand — not just the certification.

When you engage ColeHart, you're getting veteran-led delivery from founders who have worked inside federal programs at the operational level.
