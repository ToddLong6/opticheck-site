Our Philosophy

"We use AI where it makes sense not because it's a trend."

ColeHart is not an AI company. We are an operations and advisory firm that uses AI as a precision tool.

Every technology solution we build starts with a process problem, not a technology solution looking for a problem to solve. That distinction matters and it's the reason our tools actually get used after the engagement ends.

When we recommend or deploy AI in a client environment, it's because the underlying process is sound and the automation will compound that soundness not paper over a broken foundation.
