ColeHart Consulting

Federal infrastructure. Program management. Operational assessments.

ColeHart Consulting is the primary delivery engine of the firm. We work with government agencies and enterprise organizations to assess operations, modernize infrastructure, manage programs, and build resilient systems.

Core Services

What we deliver.

🔍
Operational / Infrastructure Assessment

Our flagship engagement. We evaluate your organization's operations and infrastructure, then deliver a structured roadmap with actionable recommendations. Every assessment is grounded in what we actually observe — not a generic template.

Typical scope: $5,000 – $15,000
📋
Program Management Support

Embedded or advisory PM support for federal infrastructure and technology programs. Includes risk analysis, schedule management, and stakeholder coordination — aligned with federal program environments.

Scope-based pricing · Contact for details
🖥️
IT Infrastructure Modernization

Assessment and strategy for aging IT environments. Covers network architecture, data center operations, cloud readiness, and system integration — with a practical roadmap for modernization that accounts for operational continuity.

Scope-based pricing · Contact for details
📄
Federal Proposal Support

Development support for government contract proposals including technical writing, past performance documentation, and compliance review. We help you put forward your strongest possible submission.

Scope-based pricing · Contact for details

Cybersecurity & Compliance Advisory

Gap assessments, policy development, and compliance support aligned with federal frameworks — NIST, CMMC, and FedRAMP-readiness. We identify where you are, where you need to be, and how to get there.

Scope-based pricing · Contact for details
How We Work

Structured, collaborative, and transparent.

1
Discovery first.
Every engagement begins with discovery. We spend time understanding your environment before we make recommendations — because the wrong diagnosis leads to the wrong solution.
2
Collaborative delivery.
Delivery is structured, documented, and collaborative — your team is involved throughout, not handed a report at the end. We build internal capability alongside every deliverable.
3
Transparent pricing.
Pricing is scope-based and transparent. No retainers required to start. We'll define the engagement scope clearly before any work begins, so you know exactly what you're getting.
