ColeHart Academy

Training built for people who need to apply what they learn.

ColeHart Academy builds the knowledge and capability that sustain the work we do in consulting and technology. Programs are designed for practitioners — not theory-only training that never makes it back to the job.

Training Areas

Four programs. Practical focus.

🏛️

Government Contracting Fundamentals

SAM.gov registration, NAICS codes, capability statements, proposal basics, and finding opportunities. Designed for small business owners and emerging contractors who need to move from "I want to work with the government" to actually doing it.

Ideal for: Small business owners · New contractors · Veteran entrepreneurs
📋

Project Management Essentials

Practical PM tools, risk frameworks, schedule management, and stakeholder communication — aligned with federal program environments. Not a PMP exam cram, but the skills that make you effective in real-world programs.

Ideal for: PM professionals · Federal program staff · Operations managers
🤖

AI & Technology Adoption

How to evaluate, integrate, and manage AI and automation tools in an operational context. Built for non-technical decision-makers and operations leaders who need to understand what they're buying and how to make it stick.

Ideal for: Operations leaders · Non-technical executives · IT decision-makers
🎖️

Operations Leadership Development

Leadership and systems thinking for operations managers. Covers workflow design, performance measurement, team development, and change management. Grounded in the discipline and accountability of military leadership principles.

Ideal for: Team leads · Operations managers · Emerging leaders
