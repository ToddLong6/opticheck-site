About ColeHart

How ColeHart was built — and why it matters.

Most consulting firms separate infrastructure from technology, and both from execution. That gap costs organizations time, money, and momentum. We built ColeHart to bridge all three.

Our Origin

A simple observation — and a better way to solve it.

ColeHart was founded on a simple observation: most consulting firms separate infrastructure from technology, and both from execution. That gap costs organizations time, money, and momentum.

We built ColeHart to bridge all three — combining deep federal infrastructure experience with hands-on IT operations, systems thinking, and practical AI integration.

The result is a firm that doesn't hand over a report and disappear. We work alongside your teams, understand how things actually operate, and build better systems from the inside out.

What We Believe

Three principles that shape every engagement.

🏗️

We go inside organizations.

We don't hand over a slide deck and disappear. We work alongside your teams to understand how things actually operate, then build better systems from the inside out.

🔧

Technology is a tool, not a solution.

AI and automation only work when the underlying processes are sound. We fix the foundation before adding the technology — every time, without exception.

👥

People drive everything.

Systems are built by people and run by people. We invest in helping your team grow alongside the solutions we deliver — because sustained results require capable people.

Veteran Foundation

The values that shaped our service — now shape our work.

ColeHart was founded by veterans who brought a specific set of values from military service into the business world: discipline, accountability, adaptability, and a deep respect for mission.

That foundation shapes how we engage with every client — government or private sector. We show up prepared. We deliver what we promise. We tell you the truth even when it's harder to hear.
