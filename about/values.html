What We Stand For

We carry what we learned in uniform into everything we build.

ColeHart's values are drawn from the same foundation that shaped our service — the Army Values. These aren't military-only principles. They're the standard for how any mission-driven organization should operate.

The LDRSHIP Framework

Seven values. One standard.

L
Loyalty

We stand by our clients, our partners, and our commitments through every phase of the engagement. Not just when it's easy.

D
Duty

We deliver on what we promise. No excuses, no shortcuts. Duty means showing up when it matters most — especially when it's inconvenient.

R
Respect

Every stakeholder — from the executive suite to the frontline worker — is treated with the same level of professionalism and regard. Good systems work for everyone.

S
Selfless Service

Our success is measured by your outcomes, not our invoice. If you grow, we've done our job.

H
Honor

We operate with absolute transparency. We say what we mean, we document what we agree to, and we never compromise the integrity of our work to close a deal.

I
Integrity

We give honest assessments — even when the honest answer is harder to hear. Our clients deserve the truth, not the version that's easiest to sell.

P
Personal Courage

We challenge inefficiency, advocate for necessary change, and never take the easy path when the right one is available. Growth requires that kind of courage — and so does real consulting.

Beyond the Engagement

"We care about seeing people grow."

Beyond delivering systems and reports, ColeHart is invested in the long-term development of the people inside every organization we work with.

We build knowledge transfer into every engagement so that your team walks away more capable than when we arrived. That's not a nice-to-have — it's part of how we define a successful delivery.
